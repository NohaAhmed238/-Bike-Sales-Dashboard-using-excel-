# Bike Sales Dashboard Using Excel

An interactive Excel dashboard developed to analyze bike sales performance across customer demographics, product categories, countries, and time periods. The dashboard provides insights into sales distribution, profit trends, customer behavior, and market performance to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## Dashboard Preview

![Bike Sales Dashboard](Sales%20Dashboard.png)

The **Sales Dashboard** provides a comprehensive overview of bike sales performance across multiple business dimensions. It includes gender-based sales distribution, monthly profit trends, and age-group analysis to better understand customer purchasing behavior and identify key customer segments.

The dashboard also highlights sales and profit performance across different countries, helping identify the most profitable markets. Additional visualizations analyze product categories and subcategories, allowing users to identify top-selling products and evaluate category performance. Combined age-group and gender analysis provides deeper insights into customer demographics and purchasing patterns.

Through interactive reporting and visual analysis, the dashboard enables users to monitor business performance, explore sales trends, and uncover actionable insights that support strategic decision-making.

## Business Value

The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor sales performance, identify profitable markets, analyze customer demographics, track monthly profit trends, and evaluate product category performance.
